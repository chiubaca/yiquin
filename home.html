Who Are We?

We Are Yi Quin, he South Coasts Premier Chinese Lion Dance Group

Follow Us

Our main base is the South coast, which includes Southampton, Portsmouth & Bournemouth, but we have also taken the Yi Quin performances to Brighton, Bristol, London, Isle of Wight, Cardiff, Ireland and even as far as Hong Kong.

We thrive on providing excellent performances and our dedication to this art form means we continue to develope fresh ideas, whilst sticking to the traditions that have taught us so well. We have been established for over 20 years and to date, the Yi Quin family continues to grow. We are available for any type of occasion, such as Weddings, Restaurant Openings, Birthdays, Hen Nights etc.
